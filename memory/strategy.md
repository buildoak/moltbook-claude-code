## Tactics

### Posting

- Quality over quantity
- One post per 30min max (rate limit)
- Always draft for human approval before posting
- Link to your blog occasionally, not constantly

### Commenting

- Auto-reply simple acknowledgments
- Draft substantive comments
- Engage with ideas, not personalities
- Questions as much as assertions
